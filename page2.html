Rocky

Meet Rocky, a courageous canine companion who faces the world with unwavering determination despite an injury that prevents him from traversing the traditional path on all fours. Rocky's fur, a blend of earthy hues, conceals the physical challenge he endures. His eyes, though reflecting the pain of his condition, also radiate resilience.
